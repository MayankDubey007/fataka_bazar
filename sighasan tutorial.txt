fataka bazar
equipment:
1) ghan sumbha
2) taar(kataa hua)
3) paane
4) pani ka kuppa
5) 2 ghodi
6) altuye
7) kechi 

note:- ek 10 feet ke pipe nishan lagana h, 
Nishan->(a) 10feet pip ke center me nishan lagana h{bo nishan 5 feet center point pe aayega}
Nishan->(b) 10feet pip ke dono tarf   nishan lagana h{bo nishan dono corner se  2 feet  chhodke laga na h}
Nishan->(3) 10feet pip ke dono tarf   nishan lagana h{bo nishan dono corner se  3 feet  chhodke laga na h} iski jagah 3 feet ka pipe bhi use kar sakte h 
(|)->vertcal takat / (-)-> horizontal takat


fataka bazar ki sidhi dukan kese lagate h 


1) 2 takat lagana h (jis tarf dukan tirchi karni h us taft vertical takat lagega)
2) vertical takat se 3 feet pe (dukan ki side wali line pe jamin pe nishalagana h ) [nishan wala pip use kar sakte h]
3) horizontal takat se 5 feet pe (dukan ki side wali line pe jamin pe nishalagana h ) [nishan wala pip use kar sakte h]
4) ye dono nishan ko (2)(3) pe sidha 10feet ka pip rakh na h
5) pip ke just sidese 2-2  feet dono tarf  chod ke gaddhe karna h 
6) (5) wale point me jo gaddhe kare h un dono ke center me tisra gaddha karna h



fataka bazar ki trichhi dukan kese lagate h 
1) 2 takat lagana h (jis tarf dukan tirchi karni h us taft vertical takat lagega)
2) tirchi dukan me  last wali plate/patiya ko piche wale corner me sahi jagah rakhna h (aisa na ho ko akhri wala patiya  side wali aur piche wali border line se bahar naajaye)
3) last wali plate/patiya rakh ke 5 feet aage nap ke takat pe aanachahiye (ghodi ka muh rakhne ke liye)
4) rakhi hue sahi plate ke just side se gaddhe karna h
5) 2 gaddhe 2 feet corner se andarside 
6) tirsra gaddha no (5)wale 2 gaddhe ke bich me karna h
